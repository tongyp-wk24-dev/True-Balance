Key Functions of Water in the Body

Water is essential for life and plays a vital role in maintaining overall health. It helps regulate body temperature, transports nutrients and oxygen to cells, and removes waste products through urine and sweat. Water also supports digestion, lubricates joints, and cushions vital organs. Additionally, it is crucial for chemical reactions in the body, including metabolism, and helps maintain the balance of fluids and electrolytes.
